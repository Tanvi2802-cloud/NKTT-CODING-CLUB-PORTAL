About NKTT Coding Club

NKTT Coding Club is a student-driven community that brings together curious minds passionate about programming, problem-solving, and innovation. Our aim is to create a collaborative space where students can learn, build, and grow together through coding challenges, workshops, and real-world projects.

From beginners taking their first steps in coding to advanced learners exploring new technologies, the club provides opportunities for everyone. We focus on enhancing technical skills, encouraging teamwork, and preparing students for hackathons, competitions, and career opportunities in the tech industry.
